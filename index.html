About Me

Hi my name is Naeem Wilson. For the most part, Im a maker at heart. Ever since middle school,
I've been working at the South End Technology Center, a maker space in boston. While there, I was
exposed to various forms and tools for making, such as lasercutting, 3D printing, photo and vector
editing software and such more.

Most of my time is dedicated to school, however I try to make whenever possible. A lot of projects
I work on are inspired by the games I play and the anime I watch. I also have a wierd infactuation
LEDs and putting neopixels everywhere I can...
